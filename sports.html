NBA Fantasy: Start/Sit guide for Week 5

We’re primed for a Week 5 that sets up nicely for us in terms of schedule, even with the NBA going idle on Thanksgiving Day as usual. Despite that one-day respite, we have more teams on four-game ledgers than those with three, which always increases our chances of a rewarding fantasy week. Each week, we’ll aim to highlight a pair of Start candidates for your fantasy rosters that may shape up as some of the tougher calls of the week to make, with the focus usually being on players with a start or roster rate of 50% or less in Yahoo leagues. We’ll also typically hone in on players with four-game weeks in an attempt to maximize opportunities for production.
